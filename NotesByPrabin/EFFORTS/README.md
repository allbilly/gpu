# ⚡ EFFORTS

Active projects, goals, and ongoing work. **Efforts** is the action layer of this vault — where thinking translates into doing.

## What Lives Here

- **Active projects** — notes tracking the progress, goals, and next actions of ongoing work
- **Course notes** — notes for classes and self-study tracks currently in progress
- **Goal notes** — long-term aspirations broken into milestones
- **Areas of responsibility** — recurring areas of life that need ongoing attention
- **Project logs** — timestamped entries tracking progress on specific efforts

## Lifecycle of an Effort

```
Idea captured in Buffer.md
        ↓
Project note created in EFFORTS/
        ↓
Work tracked via Kanban in Kanban Boards/
        ↓
Key insights extracted to ATLAS/ as permanent notes
        ↓
Project archived or closed when complete
```

## Status Tags

Effort notes typically use status tags in their frontmatter:

```yaml
---
status: active | paused | completed | archived
---
```

> ⚡ Atlas is where you think. Calendar is when you think. Efforts is what you *do* with your thinking.
