# 🗺️ ATLAS

The **Atlas** is the navigational core of this Obsidian vault. In the ACE (Atlas, Calendar, Efforts) framework, Atlas contains **Maps of Content (MOCs)** — index notes that link, group, and give meaning to other notes across the vault.

## What Lives Here

- **Maps of Content (MOCs)** — hub notes that collect and organize related notes on a topic (e.g., *Map of Programming*, *Map of Philosophy*)
- **Home notes** — top-level index notes that serve as entry points for a domain of knowledge
- **Concept notes** — evergreen notes defining core ideas with links to deeper dives
- **Structure notes** — notes that describe the relationships between other notes

## Why MOCs?

Unlike rigid folders, Maps of Content are **flexible and composable**. A note can appear in multiple MOCs. They grow organically as your thinking evolves, without forcing premature categorization.

## Navigation

Start from the vault `Homepage.md` in the root, which links to the primary MOCs here in Atlas. From any MOC, you can navigate outward to detailed notes, or inward to foundational concepts.

> 🗺️ Think of Atlas as the table of contents for your entire mind.
