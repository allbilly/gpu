# 🧠 Prabin's KB ~

The **core personal knowledge base** — this is the heart of the vault where permanent, evergreen knowledge lives.

## What Lives Here

- **Evergreen notes** — refined, standalone notes on concepts that hold value over time
- **Idea notes** — developed thoughts and original perspectives
- **How-to notes** — personal procedures, workflows, and processes that work
- **Topic hubs** — notes that collect thinking on a broad subject area
- **Personal principles** — values, mental models, and decision-making frameworks

## What Makes a Note "Evergreen"?

An evergreen note is:
- Written for **your future self** — clear enough to understand months later
- **Self-contained** — understandable without needing the context of when it was written
- **Linked** — connected to related notes so it doesn't sit in isolation
- **Titled as an assertion** — e.g., *"Constraints drive creativity"* rather than *"Creativity notes"

## Relationship to Atlas

`ATLAS/` contains the **index notes** (MOCs) that organize and link to the evergreen notes in here. Think of Atlas as the table of contents, and `Prabin's KB ~/` as the actual chapters.

## The `~` Symbol

The tilde (`~`) in the folder name is a personal convention indicating this is a **personal, dynamic, home-like space** — always growing, never fully finished, always yours.

> 🧠 The KB is not an archive. It is a living mind on disk — it grows, changes, and gets smarter as you do.
